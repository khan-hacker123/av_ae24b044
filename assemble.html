Step 2: Drone Assembly Instructions

Assembly Steps:

  1. Attach the frame arms and secure with screws.
  2. Mount motors at the end of each arm.
  3. Fix ESCs near each motor and wire them to the PDB.
  4. Mount the flight controller at the center (vibration-dampened).
  5. Connect IMU, GPS, and barometer to the flight controller.
  6. Wire up the battery and check all power lines.
  7. Attach propellers (note CW and CCW placement).
  8. Double-check all connections before powering up.
